Stuck in Darkness

I trace your name in the static Every silence screams you back to life I chose the chaos, chose the noise Now I’m choking on the quiet You were the calm in a collapsing world But I mistook peace for something I could replace I ran toward fire just to feel something Now all I feel is your absence I remember the way you carried your scars Like they were stories you never got to finish And I should’ve stayed, should’ve listened Instead I added another chapter you didn’t deserve If you’re out there, breathing through the wreckage I hope the night is kinder to you than I was I hope someone sees the light in your fractures The one I turned away from Every road I take still bends toward you But I burned the bridge with my own hands Now I’m stuck screaming into the fallout Of a choice I can’t undo I’d give anything to rewrite that moment To choose you over the storm I thought I needed But regret is a ghost that never leaves It just learns how to whisper your name So if you ever feel alone in the dark Just know there’s someone who never stopped caring Even if I was too broken to stay

Ghost of What I Chose Ghost of What I Chose I kept a wound because it knew my name, called it love, when it was just the same old rot I dressed in candlelight and lies, and you stood there, an angel in disguise, begging me to leave the fire behind. I chose the burn. I told myself I'd find a way to save us both, the poison, you, but some men build their gods from what they lose. I watched you turn to gold while I stayed ash, I watched you walk through doors I couldn't pass. Every heaven's got a devil at the gate, mine just wore my face, and called it fate. Now he holds what I was too numb to hold, he keeps you warm in nights that left me cold, and I'm still here, still drowning in the same dead sea I swore I'd leave, and never came. I see you laughing in some stranger's arms, the peace I could've been, someone else's calm. And I don't hate him, how could I? He's just the man I should've had the guts to trust in myself to become. So here's to indecision, here's to fear, here's to the amazing thing I let disappear. I'll haunt this house of habit till it falls, a ghost applauding love through borrowed walls.
